# Product Requirements Document (PRD)

**Project:** Lagos State Integrated Housing & Estate Portal (LSIHEP)  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Date:** 8 March 2026  
**Prepared For:** Lagos State Ministry of Housing & ICT Unit  
**Prepared By:** Prime Communications Integrated Solution (PCIS) Limited  

## Executive Summary
The LSIHEP is a centralized digital platform managing the full lifecycle of public housing and estate operations in Lagos State.

## Core Modules
1. Citizen/Tenant Portal
2. Estate & Allocation Management
3. Financials & Treasury Integration
4. Maintenance & Work Orders
5. Legal, Compliance & Documentation
6. Communication & Notifications
7. Reporting, Analytics & Transparency
8. Fraud Detection, Audit & Blockchain Ledger

## Technical Stack
- Frontend: Next.js, React
- Backend: Node.js + NestJS
- Database: PostgreSQL + Redis
- Cloud: AWS/Azure Government Cloud
- Integrations: LASRRA, NIMC, REMITA, Treasury, Lands Bureau
